Moderation Policy — Rebel Ants Builder & Contest

Rebel Ants LLC • Last updated: October 19, 2025

What we remove

  • Illegal content; harassment/hate; sexual content; personal data.
  • Copyright‑infringing or deceptive content.
  • Spam/scams; obvious vote manipulation or automated voting.

How we act

  1. Review quickly when content is reported or flagged.
  2. Hide or remove entries that break rules; disqualify repeat offenders.
  3. Adjust votes if we detect manipulation or automation.
  4. Explain briefly to the submitter when feasible.

Voting integrity

  • One reaction per emoji per device/browser per entry. That means up to five points from a single voter on one entry.
  • We may discount spikes or coordinated manipulation and may suspend accounts/devices involved.

Appeals & reports

Email RebelAntsNFT@gmail.com with a link to the entry and your reason. We’ll review; our decision is final for safety and fairness.

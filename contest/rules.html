Official Rules — Rebel Ants Weekly Contest

Organizer: Rebel Ants LLC • Last updated: October 19, 2025

Questions? Contact: RebelAntsNFT@gmail.com. Winners will later be highlighted on our upcoming Winners page.

1) Who can enter

  • Open worldwide to individuals aged 13+. If you’re under the age of majority where you live, get a parent/guardian’s permission.
  • Void where prohibited. No purchase necessary.

2) How to enter

  • Create your design in the Rebel Ants Overlay Builder.
  • Click Submit to Contest inside the builder.
  • Your entry must be your own creation and follow the content rules below.

3) What’s allowed (content rules)

  • No hate/harassment, illegal content, adult sexual content, personal data, or IP infringement.
  • Keep it safe‑for‑work and respectful. We may remove entries at our discretion.

4) Timing & duration

  • Each contest shows a visible start and end time on the contest page.
  • Duration can vary (e.g., 24 hours, 72 hours, one week, etc.).
  • Times are displayed in your local time; the server records in UTC.
  • When the countdown hits zero, the contest closes and new votes stop counting.

5) Voting — how scoring works

Voting uses five reaction emojis: 👍 ❤️ 🔥 😂 😮.

  • Each emoji click is worth 1 point for that entry.
  • Per device/browser, a voter can give up to 5 points to a single entry (one point per emoji). They may also vote on multiple entries.
  • Total Score for an entry = the sum of all emoji points from all voters.
  • We use a small local flag in your browser to limit one reaction per emoji. We also watch for obvious abuse (bots, scripts, mass VPNs) and may adjust/remove suspicious votes. See our Moderation Policy.

6) Winners

  • At closing time, the entry with the highest Total Score is the winner.
  • Ties: we break ties by earliest submission among top‑tied entries, or we may declare co‑winners at our discretion.
  • Final results are confirmed after a quick integrity review.

7) Prizes

  • Prizes can vary by contest and will be announced on the contest page or our official post for that round.
  • Possible prizes include: digital assets (e.g., crypto or NFTs), physical merchandise (e.g., T‑shirts, wristbands, stickers, toys), or recognition/bragging rights.
  • If a prize is digital, you may need to provide a compatible wallet address; if it’s physical, a shipping address. We’ll use this info only to fulfill delivery (see Privacy).
  • Winners are responsible for any taxes, gas/transaction fees, or restrictions under their local laws. Where a prize cannot legally be delivered, we may substitute or omit it.
  • We may ask for reasonable verification to confirm eligibility before awarding a prize.

8) Rights

  • You own your work. By submitting, you grant Rebel Ants LLC a non‑exclusive, worldwide, royalty‑free license to host, display, reuse for contest operations, crop/resize, and share your entry to promote the contest and winners (including reposts on social).
  • You can later ask us to remove your entry from our site; prior social posts may remain live.

9) Moderation & removal

We may remove entries that break rules or appear fraudulent. See the Moderation Policy for details.

10) Disclaimers

  • Not affiliated with X/Twitter or any other platform.

11) Changes & contact

We may update these Rules for future contests. Updates won’t change a contest already in progress unless required for safety or legal reasons.

Privacy Notice — Rebel Ants Builder & Contest

Rebel Ants LLC • Last updated: October 19, 2025

What we collect

  • Builder: overlays you create/upload.
  • Contest entries: image (URL or upload), display name, optional caption.
  • Voting: per‑entry emoji counts. On your device we store a small local flag so you can’t vote the same emoji twice on the same entry.
  • Basic logs: IP address, browser/OS info, timestamps (standard web logs).
  • Admin actions: when an admin starts/closes contests or deletes entries, we log the action and time.

How we use it

  • Run the builder and contest, show entries, count votes, and prevent abuse.
  • Highlight winners on our site and social (showing your display name).
  • Improve reliability and security.

Sharing

We don’t sell data. We share only what’s needed with our providers:

  • Hosting/CDN: Vercel
  • Key‑Value Store: Upstash KV
  • File Storage: Vercel Blob

Prizes

If a prize requires delivery we may ask winners for a shipping address or wallet address. We use that info only to fulfill the prize and then retain it only as long as needed for records or legal compliance.

Retention

  • Contest entries and final vote totals may be kept for the public archive of winners.
  • You can request removal of your entry from the site; previously posted social content may remain.

Your choices

Email RebelAntsNFT@gmail.com to request deletion of an entry or correction to your display name.

Children

The builder and contest are for users aged 13+. If you believe a child under 13 submitted personal information, contact us and we’ll remove it.

Changes

We may update this notice. We’ll change the date above when we do.

How the contest works

1) Create & submit

  • Make your combo in the Overlay Builder.
  • Click Submit to Contest inside the builder (we snapshot your canvas).
  • Choose a display name and optional caption. No wallet needed.

2) Vote

  • Use the reaction bar under each entry: 👍 ❤️ 🔥 😂 😮
  • Each emoji click = 1 point. One click per emoji per device/browser.
  • You can give up to 5 points to a single entry (one per emoji).
  • Score is the sum of all emoji points from everyone.

3) Timing

  • The countdown shows how long is left. When it hits 0, voting stops.
  • Length varies by round (hours or days).

4) Winners

  • Highest score wins; ties may be broken at our discretion (or co‑winners).
  • Prizes vary (crypto, NFTs, merch) or sometimes bragging rights.

5) Share

  • Use the X Share button on a card to post with a voting link.

6) Fair play

  • We limit duplicate reactions and monitor for abuse.
  • See our Rules & Moderation.
